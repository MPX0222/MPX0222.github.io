Peixian Ma received B.Eng. degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from Jinan University in 2023. He is now studying for a master's degree of philosophy(MPhil) under the supervision of Prof. Yuyu Luo,
at the Data Science and Analytics Thrust(DSA), Information Hub(INFO) of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Guangzhou(HKUSTGZ).

During the undergraduate life, he joined in The Data-driven Intelligent Systems Laboratory of Jinan University,
and participated in a National Key Research and Development Plan project, a Guangdong Province Natural Science Foundation project.
His undergraduate research direction is the Optimization and Application of Broad Learning System(BLS).

Currently, he is studying in Intelligent Data Science and Natural Language Process,
including Large Language Models(LLMs), In-context Learning(ICL), Agents, QA-systems, etc.
